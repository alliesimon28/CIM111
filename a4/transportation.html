To Vail:

To get to Vail Mountain, You can rent a car and drive, or take the Colorado Mountain Express shuttle service via I-70. I-70 is the most direct route to Vail. The most convenient airport to fly into is Eagle County Regional Airport.

To Telluride:

To get to Telluride Mountain, the best airport to fly into is the Montrose Regional Airport. You can take the shuttle or rent a car and drive on US-550 S for 65 miles until you reach the mountain.

To Breckenridge:

To get to Breckenridge Mountain, take I-70 West to Frisco (exit 203) from Denver International Airport. Continue south on Hwy. 9 to Breckenridge.

To Aspen:

To get to Aspen Mountain, exit Interstate 70 at Glenwood Springs and head 41 miles south and east to Aspen on Highway 82. (Glenwood Springs is 157 miles west of Denver).

To Beaver Creek:

To get to Beaver Creek Mountain, the most convenient airport to fly into is Eagle County Regional Airport. From the airport follow signs to I-70 East, proceed on 1-70 for approximately 22 minutes, take exit 167 for Avon. At the bottom of the exit, take a right around the roundabout heading South. To Steamboat Springs:

To get to Steamboat Springs Mountain, take I-70 W from Denver International Airport for 78 miles.Take exit 205 onto CO-9 N/Blue River Parkway toward Silverthorne. Continue to follow CO-9 N for about 38 miles. Then turn left onto US-40 W/Park Avenue. Continue to follow US-40 for 52 miles. Then turn right onto 6th street until you reach Steamboat Springs.

To Keystone:

To get to Keystone Mountain, travel west on I-70 from either location. Take exit 205 toward Silverthorne/Dillon. At the end of the exit ramp, turn left (east) onto Highway 6. Travel 6.2 miles to Keystone Resort.
