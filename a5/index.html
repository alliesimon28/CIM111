How To: Make Guacamole

    Ingredients
  1. 2 tbsp. finely chopped white onion
  2. 3 tbsp. (firmly packed) chopped fresh cilantro
  3. 2 tsp. finely chopped jalapeño
  4. 1 tsp. salt
  5. 3 medium-ripe hass avocados
Step 1: Grind 1 tbsp. of the onions, 1 tbsp. of the cilantro, jalapeño, and salt together in a bowl until all the ingredients are well ground.
Step 2: Cut avocados (about 8 ounces each) in half. Twist the halves to separate them and remove the pit with the tip of the knife. Dice the avocado.
Step 3: Scoop the diced avocado flesh into the bowl. Repeat with remaining avocado halves. Gently fold the avocado into the chile-onion paste, keeping the avocado pieces intact.
Step 4: Mix all the ingredients. Taste and add salt, if necessary.
Step 5: Add tomatoes, remaining 2 tbsp. of the cilantro, and remaining 1 tbsp. of the onions.
